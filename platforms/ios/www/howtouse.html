How to Use

1. Remove corrective lens (eyeglasses, contact lens)

2. Use [eye chart] held at arms-length distance to measure and take note of vision acuity

3. Click Start button

4. Bring screen close to within 1 inch of tip of nose while focusing sight on the two circles.

5. With finger on screen surface, swipe <-- or --> to adjust spacing between the two circles until they visually appear as one.

6. Focus sight on center of the circle, and move eyes to follow its movement. (Do Not touch screen during this exercise, so doing will cease movement of the circles).

7. Use application to exercise eyes for 2 minutes

8. Use [eye chart] to see improvement (white objects will be seen as reddish immediately after use, but will return to normal in a few minutes)
